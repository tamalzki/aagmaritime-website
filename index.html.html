About Us

A company that provides port agency, chartering, brokerage, and other related services and established by well-rounded and seasoned individuals equipped with knowledge and professionalism with over twenty years of experience in the shipping industry.

Our organization is made up of competent, dependable, and trustworthy individuals who are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week to assist and deliver high-quality services to our clients in compliance with international standards.

Before vessel's arrival, we ensure that our clients are well-informed of the port's situation, weather conditions, draft restrictions, and other pertinent details. Likewise, the master of the ship will be updated on the current state of the port, and all the necessary documents are ready upon its arrival.

We track ship activities round the clock and ready vessels for dispatch. Our company works well with port authorities and consignors by ensuring that we comply with all port regulations throughout the engagement.

Mission and Vision

Mission

Our objective is to provide timely and dependable maritime services to our clients to satisfy their needs. We have set out on a mission to save time, improve efficiency, and avoid loss of opportunity due to delays, high port costs, insufficient cargo loading/unloading control, and inept operations.

Also, adherence to business confidentiality is assured.

Vision

It is our vision to serve within the required level of professional services in the entire international community of exporter, importer, ship owners, charterers, brokers, traders, consignors and other stakeholders.
